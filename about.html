Don't just retweet

This is an experiment to see if we can find characteristcs of tweets that:

  • We can summarise easily
  • That correlate with the truthfulness of the tweet

Assumptions

We've taken a few intial (debatable) assumptions:

  • Number of retweets can go either way. It could mean that it's true, or false but shocking, amongst other things. But it still seesm like an important piece of information.
  • High speed of retweeting probably indicates RT without thinking - I've termed this 'velocity' and we find it using a search and comparing the first and last times within the 15 default available. High speed in this sense is 15 in less than 30 minutes, totally arbitrarily.
  • Supporting material is a good thing. It can still be lies but especially when you don't know someone, 'pix (or video) or it didn't happen' applies. We've just taken a few of the typical services people use for shaing video and pictures on Twitter - yfrog, twitpic, flickr, imgur, tweetvid or youtube.
  • Finally for now, we try to determine if the person's tweet url you entered was by the original tweeter. This makes a difference if you are looking for eye witness evidence.
